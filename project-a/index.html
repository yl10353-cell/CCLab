Family:

This creature represents family and the cycle of life. Two circles meet and travel together, then create a new circle that represents a child. As the child grows, the two original circles slowly disappear. The new circle continues moving forward, showing how life continues through generations.

Habitat:

The creature lives in a simple, open environment with a changing gradient sky. The environment sometimes produces rain, representing difficult or challenging moments in life. The background color can change randomly, symbolizing different emotional or social conditions that influence the creature’s journey.

Appearance:

The creature is made of simple circular shapes with soft colors. At first it appears as two colorful circles that move toward each other. After they interact, a new green circle appears and grows larger. Over time, the original circles shrink and disappear, leaving the new circle to continue moving forward. The simple shapes highlight the idea of growth, connection, and transformation.

Key Characteristics:

  • Formed when two circles meet and create a new circle.
  • Shows a life cycle where one generation replaces another.
  • Reacts to the viewer and changes in the environment.
  • Moves slowly and continuously across the screen.

What we know so far about Family? This project creates a generative “creature” that represents "family and the life cycle". The creature begins as two circles moving toward each other from opposite sides of the screen. When they meet, they travel together for a short time, showing companionship and connection. After this interaction, a new circle appears between them and slowly grows. The new circle represents a child or a new life created through interaction. As the child circle grows, the two original circles slowly become smaller and disappear, suggesting aging and the passing of time. The new circle then continues moving forward, symbolizing how life continues through the next generation. The idea behind this creature is that life is not isolated but formed through relationships, care, and time. The animation repeats this process to show the continuous cycle of family and generational change.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Humans can interact with this creature using the mouse. When the mouse moves close to the creature, it will slightly move toward the cursor, showing curiosity and sensitivity to its surroundings. When the viewer clicks the mouse, the background environment changes, which represents changes in life conditions that can affect the creature’s behavior.
